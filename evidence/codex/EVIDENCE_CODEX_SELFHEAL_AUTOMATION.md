## 3. INTENT CONTRACT (LAYER 1 — SEALED)

### Declared Intent

The system exists to perform **safe, bounded self-healing checks and remediations on a single host**, observing a predefined set of conditions, applying predefined corrective actions, and recording evidence of what occurred.

It prioritises:

* Safety over completeness
* Auditability over convenience
* Restraint over autonomy

### Explicit Non-Intent

The system does **not**:

* Make policy decisions
* Discover new failure modes
* Orchestrate other systems
* Perform destructive actions without safeguards
* Learn, adapt strategy, or modify itself
