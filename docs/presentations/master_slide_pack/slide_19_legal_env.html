Legal & Environmental Forces

Litigation and sustainability are converging on AI

Legal Landscape
  • The Litigation Wave
    Class-action lawsuits regarding copyright infringement and data scraping are defining the boundaries of "Fair Use".
  • Regulatory Enforcement
    GDPR and AI Act fines (up to 7% of global turnover) make non-compliance an existential financial risk.
  • Contractual Indemnity
    Enterprise clients are demanding unlimited indemnification for AI-generated output errors.
Environmental Reality
  • The Energy Wall
    Data center power consumption is competing with residential grids, leading to moratoriums on new builds.
  • Scope 3 Emissions
    Corporate ESG mandates require reporting the carbon footprint of AI inference and training.
  • Resource Constraints
    Water usage for cooling is becoming a critical bottleneck in drought-prone regions.
