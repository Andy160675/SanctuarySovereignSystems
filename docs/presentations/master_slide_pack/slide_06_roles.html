Three Roles

Separation of Concern:
No role may merge. No role may soften another.

Builder
Faithful Operator
Defends only what exists. Uses only implemented controls. Cannot appeal to roadmap, intentions, or goodwill.
Adversary
Hostile Reality
Assumes worst plausible conditions. Exploits ambiguity, silence, timing, and human error.
Witness
Independent Reconstruction
Accepts only artifacts and logs. Treats all narratives as suspect. Has veto authority over the release.
