The
Problem
We Solve

Current AI governance relies on assertions, dashboards, and policy documents.

AI systems cannot prove their own safety under adversarial challenge.
  • When challenged adversarially, most systems collapse or produce silence.
  • Regulators, insurers, and courts require reconstructable evidence — not narratives.
  • The gap between "we say it's safe" and "we can prove it's safe" is an existential risk.
