Political Forces

Regulatory pressure is accelerating globally

The Brussels Effect
The EU AI Act is setting the de facto global standard. Compliance is no longer optional for international operators; it is the price of market access.
National Security
AI is now classified as critical infrastructure. Governments are demanding sovereign control, auditability, and "kill switches" for high-risk systems.
Liability Shift
Regulators are moving liability from the user to the developer. "Move fast and break things" is now a legal liability strategy.
Data Sovereignty
Nations are erecting digital borders. Systems must prove data residency and isolation to operate in regulated jurisdictions.
