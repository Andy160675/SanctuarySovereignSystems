The Final Witness Oath

"This review can be reconstructed without me,
challenged without trust,
and survives disagreement."
The Standard If this oath cannot be made, the system is not governed. It is merely asserted.
The Reality Passing does not mean perfection. Failing does not mean rejection. Only silence is unacceptable.
The Stakes The oath is analogous to testimony under penalty of perjury. It binds the human to the evidence.
