Competitive Differentiation

Evidence-first governance is a defensible moat

The Evidence Moat
We provide a cryptographically sealed chain of custody for every decision. Proof is an asset, not an afterthought.
Vs. Competitors Ephemeral logs and "trust me" dashboards.
The Governance Moat
We enforce structural invariants (guardrails) that cannot be bypassed by the model. Safety is compiled-in.
Vs. Competitors Probabilistic "safety filters" and prompt engineering.
The Sovereignty Moat
We offer zero-SaaS dependency. The system runs on bare metal, air-gapped, with total owner control.
Vs. Competitors API wrappers and vendor lock-in.
