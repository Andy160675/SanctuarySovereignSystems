Accelerated Adoption Scenario

Major incident triggers rapid global mandates

The Catalyst 2026-2027
A high-profile AI failure causes significant physical damage, financial collapse, or loss of life. Public tolerance for "black box" systems evaporates overnight.
The Mandate Immediate Response
Regulators enact emergency frameworks. Burden of proof shifts to the operator. Systems unable to produce an audit trail are summarily shut down.
The New Standard Post-Event
Evidence-first governance becomes the license to operate. The Codex of Record transitions from "Best Practice" to "Compliance Baseline".
Scenario Probability: Moderate | Impact: High
