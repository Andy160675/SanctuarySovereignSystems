## 1. PURPOSE

This document maps established professional and industrial frameworks to the **Codex of Record (v1.0)**. Its purpose is to demonstrate that the Codex is not a novel invention, but a synthesis of best practices from project management, safety engineering, quality management, legal/ethical conduct, and supply chain integrity.

This alignment provides a bridge for auditors, regulators, and stakeholders familiar with these standards, showing how the Codex meets and often exceeds their underlying principles in a unified, evidence-first model.

## 3. CONCLUSION

The **Codex of Record** is not an isolated philosophy. It is the logical endpoint of applying proven, cross-industry principles of safety, quality, and accountability to the unique challenges of complex, autonomous, and AI-driven systems.

It unifies:

* **Project Management Rigor (PRINCE2, APM)**
* **Safety Engineering Discipline (NEBOSH, ALARP)**
* **Quality Management Proactivity (APQP, PPAP)**
* **Ethical & Professional Integrity (ILM, CIPS, BAR 100)**
* **Independent Oversight & Justice (AI Ombudsman, Supreme Court)**

By synthesizing these frameworks, the Codex provides a comprehensive, defensible, and **sovereign** model for governing systems where trust is insufficient and evidence is paramount.
